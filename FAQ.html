Frequently Asked Questions


Is the food served at McDonalds halal?

Answer: Yes, all McDonalds restaurants in Singapore are halal-certified by the Islamic Religious Council of Singapore.

How do I apply for a job at McDonalds?

Answer: View the 'Join Us' Page on the McDonalds Website to view the list of available positions and submit an online application to McDonalds.

How much are the Service Crew paid?

Answer: McDonald's Service Crew is paid on an hourly rate. The hourly rate varies with each restaurant.

What are the minimum qualifications I need to become a Restaurant Management Trainee?

Answer: You need a national ITE certificate in any discipline to apply for the position.

What are the working hours?

McDonalds offers flexible hours for their Service Crew depending on the needs of the restaurant and the availability of their Service Crew.

How do I get my money back if I was double-charged?

Check with the restaraunt to see if they are responsible. You can find the restaraunt contact information using the McDonalds Restaraunt Locator Page on their website. If not, check with your bank to rectify.

Where do I find the nutrition information for the McDonalds menu items?

Answer: Check the Nutrition Page on the McDonalds website.

Do you have vegetarian options?

Answer: No, McDonalds currently does not have vegetarian burgers or breakfast McMuffins.

Where do you get your beef from?

McDonalds has their beef sourced from Australia and New Zealand.

Does McDonalds have sugar-free syrup?

No, McDonalds does not have sugar-free syrup.

What happens if I forget my password on the McDonalds app?

Click on the 'Can't remember your password?' link on the log in page and follow the instructions sent to your registered email to reset your password.

What's MyMcDonalds Rewards?

A points-based loyalty program which sits within the McDonald's app. It allows you to earn points when transacting and redeem points on reward products.

How do I redeem MyMcDonalds Rewards?

You can browse the available rewards in the Rewards section. You can select the product you wish to claim and tap the Redeem button.

How do I set up my McDelivery account?

If you have a McDonald's app account post login, you can place a McDelivery® order without signing in. If you do not have a McDonald's app account, please download the McDonald's app and you are able to register for one.

Why can't I place an order without creating an account?

McDonalds requires account creation to enhance security and provide better service, including order tracking and personalized recommendations.
